FY2026 Strategic Priorities

Where Roblox Ads is headed

01

Prove immersive ad formats drive measurable ROI for brands

The critical test for 2026: demonstrate that immersive formats outperform traditional digital placements on completion rate, brand recall, and purchase intent. Without ROAS proof, adoption stalls. Q4 sponsorship revenue disclosures and FIFA 2026 campaign performance are the first public data points.

02

Scale programmatic access and self-serve buying via Ads Manager

Lower the barrier to entry for mid-market brands by expanding Ads Manager's self-serve capabilities — matching the buying mechanics that media teams already know from Meta, YouTube, and TikTok.

03

Grow non-endemic brand advertiser base beyond gaming & entertainment

Sam's Club, Venmo, and Snap represent the vanguard of non-endemic brand adoption. The FY2026 priority is scaling this playbook to CPG, QSR, beauty, financial services, and automotive categories.

04

Build brand safety and measurement credibility with agency holding groups

Partnerships with DoubleVerify and Google Ad Manager signal the intent to meet agency standards. The next step is Brand Suitability controls, MRC accreditation, and third-party viewability measurement — the prerequisites for incremental brand budgets.

05

Creator monetization as the supply engine — shared revenue model

Creators who build ad positions into their experiences earn a share of ad revenue. This incentive aligns supply growth with platform growth — and 27% of top-100 creators by engagement are already using Ads Manager to grow their audience.

Competitive Landscape

How Roblox Ads stacks up against attention platforms

YouTube & TikTok Incumbent rivals

Proven advertiser adoption, established measurement, massive Gen Z reach. Roblox must prove its immersive formats drive superior engagement depth — not just impressions — to earn incremental budget reallocation from these channels.

Fortnite / Epic Games Direct peer

Epic's brand partnerships and in-game experiences are Roblox's most direct comparable. Roblox's scale advantage (151M DAU vs Fortnite's ~30M peak) and UGC ecosystem differentiate — but Epic's brand execution quality sets the creative bar.

Meta & Snap AR Immersive adjacents

AR advertising on Meta and Snap targets overlapping Gen Z audiences with interactive formats. Roblox differentiates on dwell time — users spend 26 minutes per session on average vs seconds with AR activations.

MOAT

Roblox's unmatchable asset: dwell time × user trust × co-creation

The average Roblox user spends 2.4 hours per day in-platform — a depth of engagement that no social or video platform approaches. Combined with an opt-in ad model that users actively choose to engage with, and a creator ecosystem that co-builds brand experiences, Roblox offers something no traditional ad platform can: advertising that users don't resent.

Measurement Infrastructure

The analytics layer brands need to trust the platform

Brand Safety via DoubleVerify

Third-party verification partnership gives agency media buyers confidence that placements appear in brand-suitable environments — a prerequisite for Fortune 500 budget commitments.

Programmatic measurement via Google Ad Manager

Familiar buying and measurement interface for media teams. Impressions, completion rates, and CPM/CPV15 reporting consistent with other digital channels — removes the "we can't compare it" objection.

Incrementality — the open frontier

The critical gap: proving that Roblox ad exposure drives real-world purchase lift, not just in-platform engagement. Ghost Ads holdout methodology and geo-level conversion lift tests are the measurement infrastructure Roblox's ad platform still needs to build at scale.
